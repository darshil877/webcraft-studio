03 Upgrade Limits

Under the ₹1,000 Starter plan, the client is entitled to a maximum of 5 (five) upgrade requests after delivery. These must be requested via WhatsApp.

An "upgrade" is defined as any one change request — for example, changing a photo, updating text, adding a new section, or modifying colours. Complex additions (e.g. a new page) count as one upgrade.
